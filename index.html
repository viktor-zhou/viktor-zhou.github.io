Short Bio

Dongliang Zhou is a Ph.D. candidate (2018-present) in Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen / Department of Computer Science and Technology working with Prof. Haijun Zhang. His interests include fashion compatibility learning, image synthesis and generative models. He is a reviewer for IEEE TCSVT, Neural Networks, etc.

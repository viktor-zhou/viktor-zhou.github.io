Short Bio

Dongliang Zhou (IEEE Senior Member) is an Associate Research Professor at Tianjin University. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, where he also worked as a postdoctoral researcher under the supervision of Prof. Liqiang Nie. His research interests include multimedia computing, multimodal learning, and generative models. He currently serves as an Associate Editor for Applied Intelligence and as a member of the Editorial Board of Applied Soft Computing.

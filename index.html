Short Bio

Dongliang Zhou is a postdoctoral researcher at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, working under the supervision of Prof. Liqiang Nie. He obtained his doctoral degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology at the same institution. His research interests include multimedia computing, multimodal learning, and generative models. In addition to his research, he serves as an associate editor for Applied Intelligence and as an editorial board member of Applied Soft Computing. He is also actively involved in mentoring undergraduate and graduate students from Harbin Institute of Technology, Peking University, etc., in conducting scientific research.

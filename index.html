Short Bio

I am pursuing a Ph.D. at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, within the Department of Computer Science and Technology, under the guidance of Professor Haijun Zhang. My research focuses on multimedia computing, and multimodal learning. I will soon embark on a new journey as a postdoctoral researcher, joining the research group led by Professor Liqiang Nie.
